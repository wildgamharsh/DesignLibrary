Process

From Concept to Celebration

A clear, curated process tailored for couples who value detail, clarity, and beautifully orchestrated experiences.

① Consultation

Listening to your story

A dedicated design call to understand your venues, cultural needs, guest flow, and the mood you want guests to feel at every turn.

② Moodboard & Concept

Translating vision into visuals

We develop a cohesive visual direction—palettes, textures, florals, and references—mapped clearly across each event moment.

③ Design Planning

Technical meets theatrical

Detailed layouts, staging, and production plans ensuring décor integrates with lighting, AV, photography, and guest movement.

④ Setup & Execution

On-site transformation

Our décor team manages installation, quality checks, and last-minute adjustments so your venues open in perfect form.

⑤ Event Day Styling

Continuous styling support

Styling leads stay on-site for key moments—fluffing fabrics, refreshing florals, and ensuring every frame is camera-ready.
