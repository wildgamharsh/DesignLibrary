From Concept to Celebration

Consultation

We begin with an in-depth conversation about your vision, preferences, venue, and budget. This forms the foundation of our design approach.

Moodboard & Concept

Based on our consultation, we create visual inspiration boards and conceptual designs that capture the essence of your event's aesthetic.

Design Planning

Once the concept is approved, we develop detailed plans including materials, color palettes, floral selections, and spatial layouts.

Setup & Execution

Our experienced team handles all logistics, installation, and setup, transforming the venue according to the approved design plan.

Event Day Styling

During your event, we provide on-site styling services to ensure everything remains perfect throughout the celebration.
