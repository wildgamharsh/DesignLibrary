My Approach

With over 12 years of professional experience, I specialize in creating authentic, emotion-driven imagery. My work is characterized by natural light, genuine moments, and a documentary-style approach that tells your unique story.

Based in New York, I've had the privilege of working with clients worldwide, from intimate weddings to major corporate brands. I believe in building a connection with my subjects to capture images that feel true and timeless.

My style is clean, elegant, and focused on the essence of the moment. Every photograph is carefully crafted to become a lasting memory.
