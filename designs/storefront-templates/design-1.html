Co-founders • Amélie Rousseau & Luca Moreau

It began at a small Provençal chapel where the bride’s bouquet fell apart in the wind. We spent the night rebuilding it using wild roses from the hillside and lace from her grandmother’s veil. The wedding planner cried. The couple still sends us a postcard every anniversary.

That was the moment we stopped calling ourselves decorators.
