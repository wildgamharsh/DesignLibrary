About Elena

With over 12 years of experience transforming spaces into unforgettable experiences, I specialize in creating bespoke event designs that reflect each client's unique vision. From intimate garden weddings to grand corporate galas, my team and I bring creativity, precision, and passion to every project.

My approach combines contemporary aesthetics with timeless elegance, ensuring every detail—from floral arrangements to lighting design—contributes to a cohesive and stunning atmosphere. I believe that great events tell a story, and I'm here to help you tell yours.

Whether you're planning a wedding, corporate event, or private celebration, I work closely with you to understand your vision and bring it to life with meticulous attention to detail and creative flair.
