About the Organizer

I’m Ava, a New York–based wedding decorator and event designer with a passion for timeless, editorial aesthetics. I blend natural textures with curated details to create elevated atmospheres that feel both effortless and refined. Over the past eight years, I’ve led design for intimate elopements and ballroom receptions for clients across the Tri‑State area.

My process begins with understanding your story and priorities, then developing a cohesive concept—florals, stationery, lighting, and table settings—executed with trusted vendors. Whether it’s a rooftop ceremony or a black‑tie dinner, I ensure every moment feels considered and seamless.
